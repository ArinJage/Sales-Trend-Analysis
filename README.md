## Objective
To analyze sales performance over time and identify trends, high-performing products, and market-wise insights to support business decision-making.

# Tools & Technologies
- Power BI
- SQL
- Excel
- Python (optional)

## Key KPIs
- Total Sales Revenue
- Monthly & Yearly Sales Trend
- Top Products by Revenue
- Market-wise Performance

## Insights
- Certain markets generate high revenue with fewer customers, indicating premium buyers.
- Sales decline observed in specific products over time.
- Seasonal spikes noticed during festive months.
